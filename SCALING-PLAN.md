## Phase 5: Content Velocity System

### Daily workflow:
1. DataForSEO pulls new keyword opportunities
2. Score by volume × (1/difficulty) × CPC
3. Top 10 → auto-generate via Claude API
4. Human review queue for top 20% by traffic potential
5. Auto-publish low-risk comparisons
6. Build internal links between new and existing pages

### Weekly targets:
- Week 1: 50 new pages (health, education, finance categories)
- Week 2: 50 new pages (automotive, gaming, travel categories)
- Week 3: 100 programmatic pages (player stats, country data)
- Week 4: 100 alternatives pages + internal linking overhaul
