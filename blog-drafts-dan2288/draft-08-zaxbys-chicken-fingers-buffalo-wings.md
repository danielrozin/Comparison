# Zaxby's Chicken Fingers & Buffalo Wings: Full Menu and What to Order

*By Daniel Rozin | A Versus B | August 8, 2026*

Zaxby's Chicken Fingers & Buffalo Wings is a fast-casual chicken chain founded in Athens, Georgia in 1990. With over 900 locations across 17 states — primarily in the South and Southeast — Zaxby's has built its reputation on chicken tenders, bone-in wings, and a distinctive selection of sauces. If you're deciding what to order or comparing it to other chicken chains, here's what you need to know.

---

## What Zaxby's Is Known For

Zaxby's occupies the space between fast food chains like Chick-fil-A and casual dining chicken restaurants. Their differentiators:

- **Chicken fingers (called "Chicken Fingerz™"):** Their core product — breaded, fried tenders served with a signature toast and slaw
- **Sauce variety:** Zaxby's is sauce-forward; most orders come with a choice of sauce, and they offer more than a dozen options ranging from mild to extremely hot
- **Zubbz™ (bone-in wings):** Available in traditional bone-in or boneless styles, in multiple heat levels
- **The "Tongue Torch" and "Nuclear" sauces:** Both are genuinely hot; the chain has cultivated a culture around heat-level progression

---

## Zaxby's Full Menu Overview

### Signature Plates (Chicken Fingerz™)

Plates come with chicken fingers, Texas toast, crinkle fries or coleslaw, and a dipping sauce. They're the heart of the Zaxby's menu.

| 5-Piece Fingerz™ Plate | 5 | ~$12–$13 |

*Prices vary by location; Zaxby's doesn't publish a nationwide price list.*

### Wings

Zaxby's offers traditional bone-in wings and boneless wings. The bone-in variety is called **Zubbz™ Traditional Wings**.

| Item | Count | Approx. Price |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 5-Piece Zubbz™ Traditional | 5 bone-in | ~$9–$10 |
| 10-Piece Zubbz™ Traditional | 10 bone-in | ~$14–$16 |
| Zubbz™ Traditional 20-Piece Shareable | 20 bone-in | ~$25–$28 |
| Boneless Wings | 10 | ~$12 |

### Sandwiches and Wraps

Zaxby's expanded its sandwich lineup significantly in recent years:

- **Zax Chicken Sandwich:** Breaded chicken breast, pickles, special sauce on a brioche bun
- **Spicy Zax Sandwich:** Same with their spicy sauce
- **Chicken Wrap:** Chicken fingers wrapped in a flour tortilla with cheese, lettuce, tomato
- **Caesar Wrap:** Chicken fingers with Caesar dressing in a wrap

### Big Zax Snak® and Fingerz™ & Drinks

The "snack" category includes smaller portions for lighter meals — typically 3 chicken fingers with a drink option bundled in.

### Kids Meals

Kids meals come with chicken fingers (3 pieces for standard, 5 for larger), a side, and a drink. Applesauce or coleslaw replaces fries on request.

## Zaxby's Sauce Guide

Sauce selection is the most important decision at Zaxby's. The sauces are ranked below from mildest to hottest:

| Sauce | Heat Level | Flavor Profile |
|-------|-----------|----------------|
| Wimpy | None | Mild, slightly sweet; essentially a dipping sauce for heat-averse guests |
| Honey Mustard | None | Classic honey mustard — sweet and tangy |
| Ranch | None | Standard buttermilk ranch |
| Original Zax Sauce® | Mild | The house sauce — a tangy mayo-mustard hybrid that's distinct to Zaxby's |
| Zaxby's Seasoned Salt (dry rub) | None | Not a wet sauce; a seasoning option |
| Tongue Torch® | Medium | Cayenne-forward Buffalo-style heat; the most ordered "hot" option |
| Insane | Hot | Significantly hotter than Tongue Torch; habanero-based |
| Nuclear | Very Hot | The hottest standard sauce; ghost pepper-level heat for serious heat-seekers |
| Coconut | None | Sweet, tropical; available seasonally in some markets |

**The Original Zax Sauce® is the go-to recommendation** for first-timers. It's unique to Zaxby's (unlike their ranch or honey mustard) and complements the chicken without overwhelming it. Most regular Zaxby's customers default to this sauce.

## Zaxby's vs. Chick-fil-A

This is the comparison that comes up most in the Southeast, where both chains compete directly.

| Feature | Zaxby's | Chick-fil-A |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Chicken tender quality | Very good, thicker | Good, more consistent |
| Sauce variety | Larger variety, heat-level options | Fewer options; Chick-fil-A Sauce is iconic |
| Price | Slightly higher | Slightly lower |
| Bone-in wings | Yes | No |
| Sandwich selection | Smaller | Broader |
| Locations | ~900 (South/Southeast) | 3,000+ (nationwide) |
| Sunday availability | Open | Closed |
| Drive-through speed | Variable | Fast; optimized for throughput |

If you're specifically looking for bone-in wings and a range of heat levels, Zaxby's wins clearly. For chicken sandwiches alone, the comparison is closer. Chick-fil-A's waffle fries are a meaningful advantage over Zaxby's crinkle fries for most customers.

## Zaxby's vs. Wingstop

For wings specifically:

| Feature | Zaxby's | Wingstop |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Wing varieties | Bone-in and boneless | Bone-in, boneless, and flats only |
| Sauce count | 8–10 | 12+ |
| Flavor variety | Traditional range | More exotic flavors (Lemon Pepper, Cajun) |
| Sides | Fries, coleslaw, slaw | Louisiana Voodoo Fries, seasoned fries |
| Chicken tenders | Yes (core menu) | Yes (but not the focus) |
| Locations | ~900 | 1,700+ nationwide |

Wingstop is a stronger choice if wings are specifically what you want, especially for their dry-rub and specialty flavors. Zaxby's is the better choice if you want tenders plus wings in one visit, or if the heat-level progression matters to you.

The **Zax Chicken Sandwich** is the lightest option on the main menu if calories are a concern. The **Traditional Wings** provide the highest protein-to-calorie ratio when ordered without sauce (eat with sauce on the side).

## What to Order at Zaxby's: Recommendations

**First visit:** 5-Piece Fingerz™ Plate with Original Zax Sauce®. This introduces you to what Zaxby's does best.

**Wing lovers:** 10-Piece Zubbz™ Traditional Wings with Tongue Torch sauce. If you like heat, step up to Insane on your next visit.

**Sandwich eaters:** Zax Chicken Sandwich or the Spicy version. Not Zaxby's strongest category, but respectable.

**Group ordering:** The 20-piece shareable wings + a Fingerz™ Plate for variety. Both categories covered.

**Heat-seeker challenge:** Nuclear sauce. Order it with a small portion first — it's genuinely very hot and not gimmick heat.

## Zaxby's App and Ordering

Zaxby's offers mobile ordering through their app (iOS and Android). The app includes a loyalty program called **Zax Rewardz™** — points per dollar spent, redeemable for free menu items. If you visit Zaxby's more than once a month, downloading the app and enrolling is straightforward value.

The app also provides the most reliable access to current local prices, which vary by location.

## Quick Summary

- Zaxby's specializes in chicken fingers (Fingerz™), bone-in wings (Zubbz™), and a wide sauce selection with genuine heat-level variety
- Original Zax Sauce® is the house signature and the best starting point for sauce selection
- Prices run roughly $10–$13 for a full plate; wings are $9–$16 depending on count
- Zaxby's beats Chick-fil-A on wings and heat variety; falls behind on sandwich breadth and location count
- Zax Rewardz™ loyalty app is worth downloading for regular visitors
